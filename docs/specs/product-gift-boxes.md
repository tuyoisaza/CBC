# CBC Gift Boxes — Product Specification

---

## The Coffee — Lorena's Micro-Lot Curation

The coffee in every CBC box is a **specialty coffee micro-lot**, selected personally by Lorena at the time of each order. Micro-lots are small-batch coffees from specific farms (fincas), cooperatives, or regions — they change by season, by harvest, by supplier.

**This is a feature, not a limitation.** It means:
- Every box is unique to its moment
- No two orders are guaranteed to have the same coffee
- The recipient is getting something rare — not a commodity
- The Curation Card tells the story of *this specific lot*, making the gift unrepeatable

Lorena tracks available micro-lots from her network of suppliers and selects the best one for each order cycle.

## Co-Branding Spec

Company branding is included as standard in all orders — not an upsell.

**What gets branded:**
- Exterior box — company logo + CBC logo
- Optional insert card with a personal message from the company to recipients

**What the client provides:**
- Logo file (PNG or SVG, high resolution)
- Optional: message text for insert card (max ~80 words)

**Production lead time:** 7–10 business days

## The Curation Card

A signature CBC piece in every box — Lorena speaking directly to the recipient about the specific coffee they're holding.

**Includes:**
- Coffee name and origin (country, region, finca or cooperative)
- Variety (Bourbon, Typica, Geisha, etc.)
- Process (washed, natural, honey, etc.)
- Tasting notes (3–4 descriptors)
- Brew guide for the accessory in the box
- Lorena's signature / photo
- CBC logo + website

**Format:** Double-sided card, approx. 10 × 15 cm, heavy stock, black background, brand colors.

## Tier 2 — Executive Gift Boxes (Consultative Sale)

Not available for self-serve. Lorena sells these directly through conversation.

**Who they're for:** C-suite, top clients, VIP occasions where the gift needs to be extraordinary.

**What makes them different:**
- Premium brewing equipment (e.g., Kyoto cold drip, high-end pour-over)
- Lorena selects an exceptional micro-lot specifically for the recipient or occasion
- Fully custom packaging and presentation
- Price and contents defined per order

**Entry point:** Direct conversation — WhatsApp, phone, or in-person. No catalog, no fixed price.
