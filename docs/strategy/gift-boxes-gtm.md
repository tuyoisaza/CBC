## The Pivot

Courses, a physical café, and a general online coffee presence are on pause. The new CBC revenue focus is **corporate coffee gift boxes** — a proven B2B gifting category in Mexico, especially in Q4 and for client/employee appreciation throughout the year.

CBC has a natural edge here:
- **Micro-lot coffee, always rotating** — Lorena selects the best specialty micro-lot available at the time of each order, sourced from varying fincas, suppliers, and regions. No fixed SKU. Every order is unrepeatable.
- **The Curation Card** — every box includes Lorena's written description of that specific coffee: origin, finca, variety, process, tasting notes, and how to brew it. The recipient understands what they're drinking and why it's special.
- **The Experiencia tier** — the only corporate gift box in Mexico that includes a live expert class for the whole team.
- **Two sales tiers** — standard boxes for HR/procurement, and a consultative executive tier for C-suite gifting (Kyoto brew and above), sold by Lorena directly.
- A strong brand identity (premium black + yellow, handcrafted feel) with confirmed suppliers for packaging, decoration, and brewing accessories.

## The Offer

### What Every Box Includes (Standard)

- **Specialty coffee** — Lorena's current curation, the best available at the time of the order. Changes as better coffees arrive.
- **Curation Card** — Lorena's description of the coffee: origin, variety, process, tasting notes, and how to brew it. Her voice, her expertise, in every box.
- **Company branding** — logos and branding of the client company are included as standard in every order. This is not an upsell.

### Two Box Variants

Both at $799 MXN. Choice is based on brewing preference.

#### Box Prensa Francesa — $799 MXN
- Specialty micro-lot coffee (250g) — Lorena's current selection
- French press 350cc
- Curation Card (Lorena's coffee story + brew guide)
- CBC + client company co-branded box

#### Box Moka — $799 MXN
- Specialty micro-lot coffee (250g) — Lorena's current selection
- Mini moka italiana
- Curation Card (Lorena's coffee story + brew guide)
- CBC + client company co-branded box

### The 15-Box Threshold — QR Class Card
Orders of **15 or more boxes** include a QR card in every box. Lorena hosts one live session for the company's team — covering the coffee they received, its story, and how to prepare it. One session per order.

This is the differentiator. It turns a physical gift into a shared team experience — and it only activates at volume, which incentivizes larger orders.

*Minimum order: 10 boxes. Company branding included as standard in all orders.*

### Tier 2 — Executive Gift Boxes (not click-and-buy)
A separate, consultative product tier for top executive gifting. Includes premium brewing equipment (e.g., Kyoto cold drip). Price and contents defined per order. Lorena sells these directly — no catalog listing. See `docs/specs/product-gift-boxes.md` for details.

## Sales Motion

### Step 1 — Build the channel (Week 1)
- WhatsApp Business: dedicated number for gift box inquiries, automated greeting with menu/pricing
- Landing page on coffeebunncafe.com: single-focus page for corporate gifting (no courses, no distractions)
- Simple order form or Calendly for quote calls
- Instagram: 3 launch posts (product reveal, story/traceability, corporate gifting angle)
- LinkedIn: Lorena posts announcing the pivot — personal credibility drives B2B trust

### Step 2 — Direct outreach (Week 1–2)
- LinkedIn: search HR managers + Office managers in CDMX, 10–20 personalized DMs/day
- WhatsApp cold outreach to warm contacts in Lorena's network
- Email: short pitch to any existing CBC contacts/subscribers
- Target: 5 quotes sent in first week, 2 closed orders

### Step 3 — Social proof (Week 3+)
- First orders → photograph beautifully → Instagram/LinkedIn content
- Ask clients for a Google review or WhatsApp testimonial
- Show the co-branding result for any company that allows it

### Step 4 — Scale
- Referral program: corporate buyers who refer another company get a free box
- Partner with event planners and corporate gifting agencies
- Offer a "suscripción corporativa" (monthly coffee for the office) as an upsell
